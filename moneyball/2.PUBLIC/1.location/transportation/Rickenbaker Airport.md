#airport
Rickenbacker International Airport (LCK) offers scheduled passenger flights primarily through Allegiant Air. The destinations served are mostly leisure-focused and include:

- Tampa / St. Pete (PIE)
- Destin / Ft. Walton Beach (VPS)
- Myrtle Beach (MYR)
- Orlando/Sanford (SFB)
- Punta Gorda / Ft. Myers (PGD)
- Savannah / Hilton Head (Save)
- Ft. Lauderdale (FLL)
- Sarasota / Bradenton (SRQ)
- Charleston (CHS)
